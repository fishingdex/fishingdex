Privacy Policy for FishingDex

Last Updated: October 15, 2025

Introduction

FishingDex ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use FishingDex, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and username (optional, only if you create an account)
  • Fishing Data:
    • Fish catches (species, photos, location, date, weight, length)
    • Favorite fish species
    • Fishing preferences (units, language)
    • Weather conditions searches

Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: Approximate location for weather conditions (only when you use the weather feature)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Data: Features used, app interactions, crash reports

Photos and Camera Access

  • Camera: Used to photograph your fish catches
  • Photo Library: Used to select existing photos of your catches

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the FishingDex service
  • Display local weather and fishing conditions
  • Sync your data across your devices (if you create an account)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send important updates about the app (if you opt-in)
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device using:

  • AsyncStorage for preferences
  • Local database for catches and favorites

Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you create an account, your data is synced to our secure servers powered by Supabase:

  • Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Data is encrypted at rest
  • Servers are located in secure data centers

We Do NOT

  • ❌ Sell your personal data to third parties
  • ❌ Share your fishing locations publicly
  • ❌ Use your photos for marketing without permission
  • ❌ Track your location in the background
  • ❌ Share your data with advertisers

Third-Party Services

FishingDex uses the following third-party services:

Supabase (Optional - Cloud Sync)

  • Purpose: Account authentication and data synchronization
  • Data Shared: Email, username, fishing data
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

OpenWeather API

  • Purpose: Weather and fishing conditions
  • Data Shared: Approximate location (when you request weather)
  • Privacy Policy: https://openweather.co.uk/privacy-policy

Expo/React Native

  • Purpose: App framework and crash reporting
  • Data Shared: Device info, crash logs (anonymous)
  • Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You can:

  • ✅ Use the app without creating an account
  • ✅ View all your data anytime in the app
  • ✅ Export your data (JSON format)
  • ✅ Delete specific catches or favorites
  • ✅ Disable location services
  • ✅ Disable notifications

Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open FishingDex
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Scroll to Data section
  4. Tap Delete Account (if logged in)
  5. Confirm deletion

All your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

For local-only data (no account), simply uninstall the app.

Location Services

You can control location access:

  • iOS: Settings → FishingDex → Location → Never/While Using
  • Location is only used when you request weather conditions
  • We never track your location in the background

Notifications

You can disable notifications:

  • In-app: Profile → Preferences → Notifications (toggle off)
  • iOS: Settings → FishingDex → Notifications (disable)

Children's Privacy

FishingDex is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Users

FishingDex is available worldwide. If you use FishingDex outside of your country, your data may be transferred to and processed in different countries where our service providers operate.

By using FishingDex, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification (if you opted in)

Continued use of FishingDex after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@fishingdex.app
  • GitHub: github.com/yourusername/fishingdex

Data Processing Agreement

By using FishingDex, you agree that:

  • You are 13 years or older (or have parental consent)
  • You consent to the collection and processing of data as described
  • You understand your rights and how to exercise them
  • You can use the app without an account (local-only mode)
